Terms frequency analysis

As part of the text analysis of papers, including wordcloud and terms frequency analysis, we read the full list of abstracts from the papers and process them to create a tidy data structure without stop words. The stop words include specific words, such as paper and authors, which are included in many abstract, abbreviations such as e.g., and terms particular to scientific articles, such as summary. Also all numeric literas are removed from the word list. Reference book to text mining in tidy format: Text Mining with R

About 50 % (3585) of the total words (7112) in all abstracts are considered non stop words.
